Portfolio

I like building cool things. I like fixing problems even more.

PropCalc

PropCalc is a tool that creates a detailed property valuation in seconds. PropCalc was my first foray into python scripting, flask development, and front-end javascript. More a labor of love than a finished product.

ChiChomps

ChiChomps has been my biggest personal project, combining my love of Chicago and food. The first version was just a quick proof of concept. The finished version pictured consumes multiple APIs, normalizes the feed, and serves it up in a faux Material Design layout.
Stack: Python/Flask, PostgreSQL, Angular.js

Options Away

At Options Away I'm privileged to work with the some of the best developers and designers I've ever met. Here I'm a full stack developer working on a good ole LAMP stack with an Agular.js front-end.

Random Acts

Made for the Battlehack 2015 hackathon, Random Acts was inspired by "random act of kindness" communities. Random Acts was desinged to allow random individuals to request help or give help to others. All requests and thank yous in the demo were pulled from random act subreddits. The demo implements payments and text message and email notifications.
